PROFILE

A PIECE OF MY MIND

A CREATIVE PROBLEM SOLVER

Growing up in the village, all my knowledge about computers could be summarised in 3 words: Monitor, Mouse and Keyboard. Right now, chances are I know more about computers than you can comprehend, and I'm still learning.

Trends keep changing in the IT field and that's why a creative problem solver has to learn how to learn. You must value education and not school. My man, Rick Sanchez from the animated series Rick and Morty, took the words right out of my mouth when he said,

"I'll tell you how I feel about school, Jerry: it's a waste of time. Bunch of people running around bumping into each other, got a guy up front says, '2 + 2,' and the people in the back say, '4.' Then the bell rings and they give you a carton of milk and a piece of paper that says you can go take a dump or something. I mean, it's not a place for smart people, Jerry. I know that's not a popular opinion, but that's my two cents on the issue."

Computers impress me, but the human brain blows me off my feet. Comparing these two, is like comparing lanes and highways. Not even the 93 pentaFLOP(93,000 trillion calculations per second) Sunway TaihuLight, matches a third of the brain's computing power which is in the exaFLOPs.

That's why I believe the human brain can solve problems of almost all kind. I solve human problems using technology as a tool. Why technology? After prayer, its the most powerful tool.

I love Africa. Africa faces so many problems which haven't been solved yet. I have interacted with young and old people who share the same vision for this beautiful continent as I do. A vision where Africans solve their own problems effectively.

One thing I hate: bureaucracy.

WHAT CAN I DO?

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

Yes, I can chew code. I write well-documented code. Code that even my grandson won't curse me as he tries to maintain it, years after I retire. Of course, I invest a lot time in perfecting the skill.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Carrying out software projects can be a pain in 'a sensitive part of the human anatomy' if not planned and coordinated well. It takes a creative problem solver to carry out a successful software project, and that's what I am.

Q.A & TESTING

Crooks in this industry will carry out software projects that will leave you wondering who their mother is. I perform quality assurance and testing on software projects using industry-approved tools and highest professional standards.

SYSTEM ADMINISTRATION

I lose appetite and sleep when my computer systems are having issues. You need such a personality to ensure that servers, workstations and information systems work as expected, and to secure computer and network systems from "the Bad Guys".

LEARN AND GAIN

My curiosity educates me. I have no set limits to what I can learn. My biggest teacher is: Experience, learning from doing. Others include, but not limited to: Human beings, Youtube Tutorials, MITOpenCourseware, Books and Publications.

TEACH AND SHARE

You will never learn a topic better than when you start to teach it. That's what I believe. Besides, it gives me joy of making a difference in someone's life. I teach girls How to Code, for free. Why girls? The gender gap in this industry is so wide that, we shouldn't allow it to grow.
